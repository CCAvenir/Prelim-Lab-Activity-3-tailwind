About Me

Joanna Karpowicz
I’m a painter, creator of comic art, and illustrator. Since 2012, I have worked on a painting series with Anubis.

I graduated from the Faculty of Painting at the Jan Matejko Academy of Fine Arts in Cracow, where I received my diploma in the studio of Prof. Leszek Misiak. I’m related to artists: Anna Karpowicz-Westner (mother), Sławomir Karpowicz (father), Katarzyna Karpowicz (sister), and Paulina Karpowicz (cousin). I live and work in Kraków, Poland.

Artworks in Institutional Collections:

My sketches from the comic “Postcards from Białystok” and a promotional artwork for the movie “The Grain of Truth” (dir. Borys Lankosz) can be found in the collection of POLIN Museum of The History of Polish Jews.

Many of my artworks and pieces from my workshop are present in the newly opened Centrum Komiksu i Narracji Interaktywnej in Łódź, a place devoted to the history and presence of Polish comic and gaming industry.
